BatStateU BS Development Communication

What is the BS Development Communication program?

The degree program described herein shall be called "Bachelor of Science in Development Communication" (BS DevCom). This Communication Program is an area of study that concentrates on the various methods by which human beings communicate.

This curriculum focuses on how verbal and nonverbal signals are utilized to establish meaning in various circumstances through the utilization of various media platforms.

The BS Development Communication of BatStateU is a CHED Center of Development in DevCom (CMO No. 38, s. of 2015) and a Level IV Re-accredited Program by AACCUP. It's under the College of Arts and Sciences, Pablo Borbon Campus.
